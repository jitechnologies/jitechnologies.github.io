Welcome to JITech — Janya Instrumentation Technologies.


Based in Bangalore, India, was established in 2019 with the primary aim of providing sales and service support for testing instruments in the fields of polymers, composites, rubber, and related industries. Founded on the philosophy of Reliability and Ultimate Customer Satisfaction, we continue to uphold these values in everything we do.
With over 20 years of experience in instrumentation, our team has been deeply involved in servicing and supporting scientific instruments with dedication and expertise. Even in challenging times, JITech has remained committed to its customers, offering support through telephone consultations and remote assistance—driven by a deep passion for the industry and a strong sense of responsibility.
We specialize in delivering high-quality service solutions for plastic and rubber instrumentation machines. Our mission is to help industries operate at peak efficiency through reliable, precise, and innovative instrumentation services.
Our approach is built on trust, transparency, and technical excellence. Whether you're a growing enterprise or an established industry leader, JITech is your trusted partner in driving performance, ensuring reliability, and supporting your long-term success.

At JITech, we believe that precision drives progress.

With a commitment to excellence, a passion for technology, and a dedication to our customers' success, we are here to support industries in every step of their growth journey.
