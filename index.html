About

Find Out More About Us

Welcome to JITech — Janya Instrumentation Technology.


Based in Bangalore, India, we are a passionate team dedicated to delivering high-quality service solutions for plastic and rubber instrumentation machines. Our mission is to help industries operate at peak efficiency through reliable, precise, and innovative instrumentation services.
With a strong foundation in technical expertise and customer commitment, JITech has been supporting businesses with advanced maintenance, calibration, and optimization services. We understand the critical role that accurate instrumentation plays in manufacturing processes, and we are here to ensure your operations run smoothly and efficiently.
Our approach is built on trust, transparency, and technical excellence. Whether you are a growing enterprise or an established industry leader, JITech partners with you to drive performance, reliability, and growth.

At JITech, we believe that precision drives progress.

With a commitment to excellence, a passion for technology, and a dedication to our customers' success, we are here to support industries in every step of their growth journey.

Partner with us — and experience the difference that expertise and trust can make.
